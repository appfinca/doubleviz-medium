FAQ

Q

Do I have to publish a post found by DoubleViz in my publication?

No. You can review the posts recommended by DoubleViz before they get published and remove the ones you don’t like. You have full control of what to post in your own publication.

Q

How exactly does the AI find the matching stories/publications for me?

DoubleViz first analyzes your content using the Deep Learning (DL) and Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques so to understand your writing styles, interested subjects, and other factors. It then finds stories with similar style and/or related subjects that can enrich your publication. Similarly, it cross-posts your stories in other publications having the right audience to create genuine reads and followers for you.

Q

Do I have control over the AI?

Yes. Although DoubleViz automates things by default, you can still opt into a lot of controls. For example, you can tell us your precise topics using tags to help the AI finds better stories/publications for you. You can set a blacklist of words (such as the names of your competitors) to avoid unwanted stories/publications. You can review the posts found by DoubleViz and remove the ones you don't like before they get published in your publication. You can also tell DoubeViz when to publish new stories such that it fits your schedule.

Q

How do my stories look in other publications?

It depends on the owner of the publication where a story is published. The owner cannot modify your original title nor content but can prepend/append words to the title (such as “Guest Post: ”) and write a paragraph as an introduction shown before your content. Similarly, you can do the same to the stories in your publication.

Q

Does DoubleViz do anything good to SEO?

Maybe. When cross-posting your story in other publications, DoubleViz makes sure your original story is the single source—each story posted by DoubleViz is added a canonical link to its source. This may create positive effects on SEO. However, please don’t take DoubleViz as an SEO tool since it is not optimized for increasing backlinks. DoubleViz is optimized for creating genuine reads for you.
