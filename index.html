How Does It Work?

1.

When you’re stuck in writing, DoubleViz pops up relevant articles trying to inspire you.

2.

If you like an article found by DoubleViz, mention it in your story and get a reward token.

3.

DoubleViz then turns the token into a mention of your stories by showing your stories to other writers when they’re stuck.

4.

Now, your visibility has double the power—the more inspiring your stories are, the more mentions and additional exposure you can get from the others.

FAQ

Q

How exactly does the AI find inspiring stories for me when I write?

DoubleViz first analyzes your current writing and past content using the Deep Learning (DL) and Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques so to understand the context, your writing styles, interested subjects, and 300+ other factors. It then finds stories that may complement or enrich your current writing. So, you can read and get inspiration from them easily.

Q

Do I have control over the AI?

Yes. You can enter keywords in the DoubleViz popup window to specify the next topics you are going write. DoubleViz then brings you inspiring stories that matches the keywords as well as your current context.

Q

How do I “mention” a story that inspires me?

You don’t need to mention every story found by DoubleViz. But if there’s one that you really appreciate, you put a link (provided by DoubleViz) to the story in your current writing. Other than this, you have full control of what to write. For example, you can surround the link with supporting text. You can also thank the author and make his/her name explicit.

Q

Can I see how other writers mentioned my stories?

Yes. DoubleViz has a “stats” page showing this and more performance metrics, including the extra reads and clicks you get from using DoubleViz.

Q

Does DoubleViz do anything good to SEO?

Probably. When a writer mentions your story, you get a “backlink” that contributes to the ranking of the story in a search engine. The more inspiring your stories are, the more effectively DoubleViz can add values to the SEO for you.
