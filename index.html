FAQ

Q

Do I have to publish a post recommended by DoubleViz?

No. You can review the recommended posts before they get published and remove the ones you don’t like. You have full control of what to post on your own blog.

Q

How exactly does the AI find the matching articles/blogs for me?

DoubleViz first analyzes your content using the Deep Learning (DL) and Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques so to understand your writing styles, interested subjects, and other factors. It then recommends guest posts with similar style and/or related subjects that can enrich your blog. Similarly, it posts your articles on other blogs having the right audience to create genuine reads for you.

Q

Do I have control over the AI?

Yes. Although DoubleViz automates your growth and saves your time, you can still control a lot of things. For example, you can tell us your precise topics using tags so the AI can make better recommendations and/or post your articles on better matching blogs. You can set a blacklist of words (such as the names of your competitors) to prevent the containing posts from being recommended. You can review the recommended posts and remove the ones you don't like before they get published. You can also tell DoubeViz when to recommend new guest posts such that it fits your schedule.

Q

How do my posts look on other blogs?

It depends on the owner of the blog where a post is published. The owner cannot modify your original title nor content but can prepend/append words to the title (such as “Guest Post: ”) and write a paragraph as an introduction shown before your content. Similarly, you can do the same to the recommended guest posts on your blog.

Q

Does DoubleViz do anything good to SEO?

Probably. DoubleViz creates backlinks to your blog when guest-posting your articles on other blogs. This creates positive effects on SEO. However, please don’t take DoubleViz as an SEO tool since it is not optimized for increasing backlinks. DoubleViz is optimized for creating genuine reads of your articles.
